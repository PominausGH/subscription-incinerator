# Set-and-Forget Promotion Plan

**Date:** 2026-01-14
**Status:** Ready for implementation
**Automation Level:** 90%
**Monthly Time Commitment:** ~10 hours

## Overview

Automation-heavy promotion strategy for 4 apps. Designed for developers who hate the social selling grind but want results.

### Apps & Status

| App | Type | Status | Primary Target |
|-----|------|--------|----------------|
| TimerForge | SaaS (Stripe) | Ready | Marketers, content creators |
| FakeCallApp | Play Store | Ready | General users |
| CV_matcher | Consulting | Waiting for feedback | HR, recruiters, founders |
| Subscription Incinerator | SaaS | In development | Budget-conscious consumers |

### Philosophy
- Batch everything, schedule everything
- Let ads do the heavy lifting
- SEO over social engagement
- AI-assisted content creation
- Check metrics weekly, not daily

## Video Strategy

### Batch Recording Setup
- Ring light + phone tripod
- Quiet room with good lighting
- Simple backdrop (plain wall or branded banner)
- Record in vertical (9:16) for TikTok/Reels
- Record in horizontal (16:9) for YouTube

### FakeCallApp Video Ideas
Record all in one session:
1. Escaping a bad date
2. Getting out of boring meeting
3. Avoiding salesperson at mall
4. Parent escaping kids' event
5. Student leaving class early
6. Awkward family dinner escape
7. Dodging a chatty neighbor
8. Leaving a party early

### TimerForge Video Ideas
1. "How to add countdown to your landing page" (tutorial)
2. "Boost conversions with urgency timers" (education)
3. "TimerForge vs free alternatives" (comparison)
4. "Set up a webinar countdown in 2 minutes" (quick tip)

### Video Editing Workflow
1. Import all clips to CapCut (free, mobile-friendly)
2. Add trending sounds/music
3. Add captions (auto-generate)
4. Export and upload to scheduling tool
5. Write captions with hashtags
